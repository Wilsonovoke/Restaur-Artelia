About us

About Us

We are Connecting the Dots in Fashion & Design

We don’t just make clothes—we preserve memories. From custom tailoring to training workshops, we help you look good while caring for your wardrobe..

Our Mission

At Restaur Artelia, our mission is to preserve the value of clothing by merging craftsmanship, creativity, and sustainability. We believe garments hold more than fabric – they hold memories, confidence, and identity. That’s why we are dedicated to creating custom pieces tailored to each individual’s unique style, while also offering high-quality repairs and reworks that extend the life of existing wardrobes. Beyond our services, we are committed to empowering individuals with the skills to care for, maintain, and even create their own garments. Through our training, we encourage sustainable fashion practices that reduce waste and celebrate personal expression.

Our Vision

Our vision is to redefine how people connect with their clothing by promoting a culture of longevity, creativity, and sustainability. We aim to become a trusted household name in garment care and customization, inspiring people to see clothing as an investment rather than something disposable. By blending tradition with innovation, we envision a future where every garment has a second chance, and every person feels confident in both their style and their ability to maintain it. Through community workshops, personalized designs, and expert craftsmanship, we aspire to lead the movement for sustainable fashion and conscious living in the UK and beyond.
